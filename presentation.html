🔄 System Flow

How the platform works — step by step

1

Registration & Verification

User registers as Investor / Farmer / Landowner → email verified → Admin approves Farmer & Landowner accounts.

2

Land Listing

Landowner registers land (size, location, photos, documents) → Admin approves → Land visible in marketplace.

3

Farm Creation (Land + Farmer)

Landowner hires a Farmer → lease payment processed → Farm entity created linking Land + Farmer + Crop Type.

4

Investment (Investor + Trees)

Investor browses marketplace → selects trees (e.g. 5 Mango trees @ 5,000 PKR each) → ownership tracked per investor.

5

Tree Management & Growth Logs

Farmer uploads timestamped photo logs (Seedling → Sapling → Mature) → investor sees real-time updates on dashboard.

6

Harvest & Returns

Farmer records harvest (yield, weight, date) → system calculates each investor's share → payout confirmed.

Why PostgreSQL over MongoDB? — Our data is highly relational. Investors own specific plants in specific farms on specific lands managed by specific farmers. That chain of relationships demands referential integrity that only a relational DB guarantees.
