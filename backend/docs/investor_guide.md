# Investor Guide: Agro-Investment Platform

Welcome to the future of agricultural investment. This guide will walk you through the process of sponsoring plants, tracking growth, and managing your harvests.

## 1. Getting Started
- **Account Creation**: Register as an "Investor" using your email and a secure password.
- **Profile Completion**: Update your full name and contact information.
- **KYC Verification**: Upload a valid ID to verify your identity. This is required for financial transactions.

## 2. Browsing Investments
- Navigate to the **Marketplace** to see available farms.
- Use filters to find specific **Crop Types** (e.g., Avocado, Coffee) or locations.
- Click on a farm to see detailed information:
  - Farm history and certifications.
  - Expected yield and harvest cycles.
  - Land and maintenance fees.

## 3. The Investment Process
- **Select a Plant**: Choose a specific plant or tree within a farm.
- **Choose Duration**: Select how many months you wish to sponsor (subject to crop cycle).
- **Payment**: Securely pay the initial land fee and maintenance fees.
- **Confirmation**: Receive an investment contract and a unique QR code for your plant.

## 4. Monitoring Your Growth
- Use your **Dashboard** to see a timeline of your active investments.
- **Activity Feed**: Receive photos and updates from the farmer (e.g., "Flowering stage", "Fertilizer applied").
- **Visit the Farm**: Schedule a visit through the booking system to see your plant in person.

## 5. Harvest & Returns
- **Harvest Notification**: You will be notified when your plant is ready for harvest.
- **Outcome Selection**: Choose to either have the harvest shipped to you or sold on the marketplace.
- **Payouts**: If sold, your share of the profits will be credited to your account after platform commissions.

## FAQ
- **Can I cancel an investment?** Only before it is finalized or during the pending stage.
- **What happens if a plant dies?** Review our "Risk Mitigation" policy in the Terms of Service.
- **How do I withdraw funds?** Go to the Billing section to request a withdrawal.
