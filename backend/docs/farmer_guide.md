# Farmer Guide: Agro-Investment Platform

Empowering farmers through direct investment. This guide helps you manage your farms and keep investors updated.

## 1. onboarding
- **Register**: Sign up as a "Farmer".
- **Farm Profile**: Create a detailed profile for your farming operation.
- **Verification**: Complete your KYC and property verification to start receiving sponsors.

## 2. Managing Farms
- **Register Land**: Work with land owners to list certified plots for cultivation.
- **Add Plants**: List specific trees or plant batches available for sponsorship.
- **Set Pricing**: Define land lease fees and monthly maintenance costs.

## 3. Daily Operations
- **Log Activities**: Regularly update your dashboard with activities:
  - Irrigation & Fertilizer application.
  - Milestone photos (e.g., First sprout, Flowering).
- **Communication**: Respond to investor inquiries and farm visit requests.

## 4. Farm Visits
- **Calendar Management**: Set your availability for farm visits.
- **Check-ins**: Use the platform to verify investor confirmation codes during visits.

## 5. Harvest Management
- **Record Harvest**: Log the quantity and quality of produce collected.
- **Fulfillment**: Coordinate with the platform for shipping or marketplace listing.
- **Get Paid**: Receive your maintenance fees monthly and a share of the harvest profits upon completion.

## Best Practices
- **Consistency**: Regular photo updates increase investor confidence and future sponsorships.
- **Transparency**: Log both successes and challenges (e.g., pest control efforts).
- **Quality**: Adhere to the cultivation standards defined for your crop type.

## Troubleshooting
- **Cannot upload photos?** Ensure they are in JPG/PNG format and under 5MB.
- **Delayed Payments?** Check your billing section and ensure your KYC is current.
