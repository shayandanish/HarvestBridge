# Landowner Guide: Agro-Investment Platform

Monetize your idle land by connecting with experienced farmers and eager investors.

## 1. Registration
- **Sign Up**: Create an account as a "Landowner".
- **Verification**: Complete KYC to ensure secure payouts.

## 2. Listing Your Land
- **Property Details**: Provide the address, total area, and soil type of your plot.
- **Documents**: Upload ownership deeds or lease authorization for verification.
- **Photos**: Provide high-quality photos of the land to attract farmers.
- **Coordinates**: Pin the exact location for mapping and investor transparency.

## 3. Partnering with Farmers
- **Verification Process**: Platform admins verify your land before it becomes available.
- **Farmer Matching**: Farmers can browse verified land and propose farm listings.
- **Lease Agreements**: Agreements are handled through the platform to ensure fair compensation.

## 4. Financials
- **Lease Fees**: Receive initial fixed payments when a plant is sponsored on your land.
- **Payouts**: Track all income through your Landowner Dashboard.
- **Tax Statements**: Access annual reporting for your lease income.

## FAQ
- **Can I list multiple properties?** Yes, you can manage a portfolio of land through your dashboard.
- **Who manages the farming?** Certified farmers handle all agricultural activities.
- **What is the platform's commission?** A standard platform fee is applied to the land lease processed through our system.
