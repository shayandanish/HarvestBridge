# Admin Guide: Agro-Investment Platform Management

A comprehensive manual for platform administrators to ensure operational excellence and security.

## 1. Dashboard Overview
- **Platform Health**: Monitor total users, active investments, and system revenue.
- **Pending Actions**: View a summary of items requiring immediate attention (Verification, Disputes).

## 2. User Moderation
- **User List**: Search and filter users by role or status.
- **Moderation Actions**: Suspend or activate accounts based on compliance.
- **KYC Review**: Access the unified verification queue to approve or reject user identities.

## 3. Approvals Workflow
- **Land Verification**: Review property documents and list land for farmers.
- **Farm Listing Approval**: Ensure farm descriptions and expected yields meet platform standards.
- **Content Moderation**: Review photos and activity logs posted by farmers.

## 4. Dispute Resolution
- **Conflict Management**: Mediate disputes between Investors and Farmers.
- **Evidence Review**: Analyze attachments and timeline logs to reach a fair resolution.
- **Formal Resolution**: Document outcomes to close disputes and notify parties.

## 5. Platform Configuration
- **Settings**: Adjust platform commission rates and operational constants.
- **Email Campaigns**: Use the Campaign Center to communicate with specific user segments.
- **Activity Logs**: Audit the trail of administrative actions for compliance.

## Troubleshooting
- **Failed Payments**: Use the Payment Monitoring tool to retry or refund transactions.
- **System Logs**: Access detailed technical logs for debugging platform errors.
